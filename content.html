Appetizers

TORSHI

Torshi is usually made by marinating chopped carrots, cabbages, cucumbers, and some herbs in a mixture of salt, pepper, and vinegar and leaving them for a few weeks.

KASHK-E BADEMJAN

Kashk e Bademjan is a traditional Iranian food containing eggplants, Kashk, and onions, making it a great vegetarian option

Main course

irani Mutton biryani

Iranian main dishes are combination of rice, meat, vegetables and nuts. Rice is a central and ancient staple of the modern Iranian Kitchen. Traditional Irani Biryani recipe is layered with fried mutton pieces . Topped with fried onions, cashews and almonds

tahdig

Tahdig is a classic Persian crunchy fried rice recipe. This delicious recipe is made in a pot with a layer of basmati rice mixed with yoghurt, saffron, spices and herbs.

khoresh-e fesenjan

Khoresh-e fesenjan is a delicious Iranian stew, which is made with the rich flavour of ground walnuts and pomegranate molasses

Baghali Polo

Baghali Polo is a classic rice dish, which can be prepared with rice, fava beans and dill
